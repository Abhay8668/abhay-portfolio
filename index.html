Who I Am

From Pharmacy
to Marketing Mastery

My journey started in a pharmacy lab — formulating ibuprofen lozenges, understanding molecular behavior, and thinking in systems. That analytical foundation became my greatest marketing asset.

"The best marketers understand their audience deeply. I started by understanding what heals them."

At Renix Healthcare, I moved from formulations to field — managing relationships with 200+ doctors, tracking prescription behaviors, and building product visibility in a competitive pharma landscape. That's where I discovered that marketing is fundamentally about trust, timing, and data.

Now pursuing my MBA in Marketing at SIBM Nagpur, I'm combining that healthcare precision with modern digital strategy, AI workflows, and brand thinking — building a profile that sits at the intersection of domain expertise and marketing innovation.
